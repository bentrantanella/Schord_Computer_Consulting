Calculators

Welcome to our calculator page, where we offer free web-based running and racing tools for you to use. No registration necessary, just calculate and get going! These are all fairly straightforward, but if you have any questions, or if there is a calculator you'd like us to develop, send us an email at info@schordcomputerconsulting.com

Lap Split Calculator

Race Conversion Calculator

Input race distance with time, along with

based on pace of the first
