About

Founded in 2022 by Ben Trantanella, a computer science major (with a mathematics minor) and competitive runner at Union College in Schenectady, NY, Schord Computer Consulting has one mission, and that is to help you solve your computer programming and design needs. Whether it be website design, applet programming in Python or Java, graphic processing, or tutoring for high school and college students studying computer science, Schord Computer Consulting is here for you. In addition, we have created a number of free calculators on our website aimed at helping competitive track runners plan, analyze, and goal set throughout their seasons.
